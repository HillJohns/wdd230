Target Audience

Persona 1: Jane Jones

  • Age: 33
  • Income: $30-$36K
  • Experience: Beginning a bachelor's degree in Business Management
  • Family: Married with young children
  • Device: Phone, tablet, and latptop

Scenarios: Jane is a stay at home mom who has dreamed of owning her own small baking business to help support her family from home. She started selling baked goods on social media to friends and family, but is looking to expand. Her next step is to get a local business license. She is also looking for support from like-minded business owners in the area. Jane has been browsing websites hoping to find an online community where she can search for answers, ideas, and ask questions.

Persona 2: Bob Sperry

  • Age: 60
  • Income: $89-99K
  • Experience: 20+ years in business, 10+ years in local politics
  • Family: Married, father and grandfather
  • Device: Desktop

Bob is a local businessman who has watched the city of Nephi expand by a great deal over the years. He wants to promote more growth both for the city, and his own small empire. He has decided to sponsor local teams and high-school events in order to have his name become more recognized. Bob also wants to take part in helping other entrepenuers scale up their businesses to promote economical growth. Bob knows how critical the internet is in the current business climate, so he is stepping out of his comfort zone and onto an online platform. It is important to him that a website is straightforward and easy to navigate.
