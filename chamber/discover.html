Discover the City of Nephi

Welcome to Nephi City! Find everything that we have to offer. Local attractions range from the famous Ute Stampede Rodeo to the newly rennovated City Park, Pool, and Splash Pad! Our community offers a range of youth sports and activities with a brand new Rec-Center, including a lap pool, coming soon.
The surrounding area boasts sites such as Mt. Nebo Scenic Loop, Yuba State Park, and endless canyon and mountain trails with stunning views.
